Time Cards Management System



Timecards management system helps a company's manager or HR Department to calculate the salary of the employees in a easy manner. This software needs the employees to check-in when they enter the company and checks-out when they leave from work and allows the employees to request for either a sick leave or a casual leave. And the HR Department can calculate the salary of employees easily.
